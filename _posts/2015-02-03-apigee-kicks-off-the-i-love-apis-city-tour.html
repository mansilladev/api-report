Apigee®, developer of an intelligent API platform for digital business acceleration, today kicked off the "I Love APIs" City Tour -- a series of free, one-day events held across the U.S. for digital business and technology leaders in every industry. Starting today in Chicago, The "I Love APIs" City Tour will provide strategic and technical best practices for delivering data-driven API programs that can drive mobile app strategies, enable new revenue opportunities, and foster digital innovation.

"I Love APIs" City Tour events will feature leading organizations sharing their real-world challenges, opportunities, and lessons learned on their paths to becoming digital businesses. Companies participating include Cars.com, First Data, Fox Broadcasting Company,McCormick, and Walgreens.

The "I Love APIs" City Tour will be held in the following cities:

  • Chicago: February 3, 2015
  • Atlanta: February 5, 2015
  • New York: February 12, 2015
  • Los Angeles: February 19, 2015
  • Dallas: February 24, 2015

Each "I Love APIs" City Tour event will include:

  • Morning business track: Attendees can gain an understanding for what it takes to develop a successful digital business strategy using APIs and data.
  • Afternoon technical track: Attendees can learn about modern architectures and how to implement an API-first strategy.
  • Evening networking reception: An opportunity to connect with other attendees at a reception including drinks and hors d'oeuvres.

"The 'I Love APIs' City Tour, brings much of the rich content, peer learning and networking benefits of our annual 'I Love APIs' conference out on the road," said Denise Persson, Apigee chief marketing officer. "Speed is critical to today's digital businesses, and we want to give every organization the opportunity to learn how they can quickly accelerate their data-driven mobile and digital initiatives with APIs."

Apigee is also hosting "I Love APIs" Europe 2015 -- one of the world's largest conferences dedicated to API strategies and digital business -- on March 24, 2015 in London. "I Love APIs" Europe 2015 is a one-day conference and will feature tracks for business leaders, IT leaders and app developers.
