HYFN, a leading ad technology company and full-service digital creative agency, announced today the ability to sell Pinterest advertising via the new Pinterest Ads API, as a Marketing Developer Partner (MDP).

"We couldn't be more excited to add Pinterest to the portfolio of platforms supported by HYFN," said Morgan Harris, HYFN's CEO and Founder. "Pinterest presents breakthrough opportunities to leverage compelling creative to engage audiences in amazing new ways, and HYFN is perfectly positioned at the intersection of great creative and powerful digital media buying."

HYFN's offering is unique, as the company is not only a full-service digital creative agency, but also a social ad technology provider. HYFN’s Pinterest Ads API solution is designed to make it easy for marketers to create Pins en masse for promotion leveraging the full breadth of targeting capabilities, with rich interactive reporting enabling real-time analysis.

About HYFN
HYFN is a digital technology, creative, and strategic agency that delivers solutions and social amplification at scale via its proprietary HYFN8 platform. Founded in 2000 with offices in Los Angeles, New York, San Francisco and Chicago, HYFN is a client services specialist that delivers results-driven campaigns for global, Fortune 500, and midsize companies that want to reach and engage their target audiences wherever they are, on any device.
