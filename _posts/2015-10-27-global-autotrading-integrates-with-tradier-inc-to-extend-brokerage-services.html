Tradier Brokerage, Inc., today announced that Global AutoTrading has integrated with the Tradier API to extend brokerage capabilities for the autotrading community. As part of the collaboration, investors already relying on Global AutoTrading’s service to quickly act on trade newsletter alerts will gain the ability to automatically submit orders to Tradier Brokerage, Inc. to swiftly and cost-effectively execute a trade.

“Our integration with Tradier allows us to simplify the execution of an equities or options trade,” said Jimmy Talbot, President, Global AutoTrading. “With the unbundling of brokerage services that Tradier provides, we are able to grow our user base and more effectively empower traders with real-time intelligence and more effective trading tools.”

Key Benefits to the Autotrading Investor Community

  • Choice – Ability to select multiple newsletters to be traded from a personal account
  • Flexibility – Freedom to allocate different levels of capital to each newsletter
  • Transparency – Monitoring capabilities provide visibility into trading activity, trade confirmations, weekly and monthly reports, and customer account statements
  • Integration – Autotrading can be conducted from existing brokerage accounts
  • Speed – Users benefit from the fastest email alert processing time, allowing for the best fill prices before other traders have time to enter their orders

“Global AutoTrading users represent a growing brigade of innovative traders demanding real-time, intuitive and more flexible trading solutions,” said Craig Russell, Chief Evangelist and SVP of Product at Tradier, Inc. “Our collaboration with Global Autotrading extends our ability to further arm traders with choice, flexibility, and ease-of-use when it comes to brokerage services.”

About Global AutoTrading
Global AutoTrading provides automated trading ("autotrading") of investment newsletters to its clients, allowing clients to quickly act on newsletter alerts while they sleep, work or play. With the integration of Tradier's API, Global AutoTrading is able to quickly and reliably submit orders to Tradier Brokerage, Inc. as communicated by newsletter alerts.
