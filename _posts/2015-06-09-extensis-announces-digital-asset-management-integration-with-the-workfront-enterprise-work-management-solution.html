Extensis, a global provider of digital asset management (DAM) solutions, announced today a partnership with Workfront (formerly AtTask), the leading provider of cloud-based Enterprise Work Management solutions. The companies are partnering to integrate Extensis Portfolio® into the Workfront Enterprise Work Cloud, enabling customers to access their digital assets directly within their marketing and creative workflows.

Digital content is cornerstone to the work of marketing and creative organisations and teams. Companies across the globe use Extensis Portfolio to manage libraries that can include millions of digital assets— including photos, graphics, documents, video and audio files—thereby saving thousands of dollars every year in time and direct costs.

By linking the Extensis and Workfront systems using Portfolio’s open API, users can pull digital assets into their larger workflow, enabling a comprehensive and highly efficient solution for managing and completing work from start to completion.

“Extensis and Workfront have a shared interest in eliminating the chaos that often ensues in the marketing and creative workflow,” said Toby Martin, Director of Product Development at Extensis. “In bringing our systems together, we’re further streamlining the process so teams can collaborate on projects and digital content from a central location.”

"Both Workfront and Extensis have seen rising interest from customers to integrate our solutions so digital assets can seamlessly fit into the work management lifecycle,” said Ed McGarr, Director of Partnerships and Alliances, Workfront. “This integration will amplify our mutual mission to help companies streamline process so they can set their creativity free and focus on delivering on their business objectives.”

For more information about Workfront, visit http://www.workfront.com. To learn more about Extensis digital asset management solutions, visit http://www.extensis.com/portfolio.
