Tradier Brokerage, Inc., the first open-brokerage API company, today announced that web and mobile application,Technician, powered by ChartIQ, has integrated with the Tradier API to enable trades on the go through the portal from smartphones, tablets or the web, in addition to in-depth technical analysis. Technician’s free charting and technical analysis platform is designed for the modern, mobile world of trading, and with the Tradier Brokerage integration, it now includes the ability to submit trade orders from anywhere and from any device.

Technician combines real-time data with in-depth analytical horsepower and intuitive mobile design to provide modern traders with technical analysis and trading experiences that's fit for the fast paced, modern world. Tradier further empowers traders by providing fast and reliable access to brokerage resources all from one, comprehensive tool.

"Modern traders require a technical analysis and trading platform that is in-sync with the speed the market requires and provides access to resources efficiently, anytime, anywhere," said Dan Schleifer, co-founder and CEO of ChartIQ. "The integration with Tradier Brokerage highlights our ongoing dedication to elevating trading experiences for all.”

“ChartIQ’s tool, Technician, meets the growing demand for flexible and real-time solutions that can be accessed at easy convenience for modern, mobile-enabled traders,” said Craig Russell, Chief Evangelist and SVP of Product at Tradier, Inc. “Technician’s integration with our brokerage API provides today’s innovative community of traders with more freedom and flexibility to access brokerage services and helps them go from idea to action in seconds.”

Technician provides access to 85+ indicators, various chart types, drawing and annotation capabilities and includes cross-device syncing of views and watch lists, StockTwits and Yahoo Finance news integration and free real-time data for US equities and forex pairs. Download Technician for iPhone or iPad at the Apple App Store, for Android phones and tablets on Google Play, or access it on any device through the web app.

To learn more about the integration and benefits of the Tradier and Technician relationship, visit https://brokerage.tradier.com/platforms/technician
