Hyland, creator of OnBase announced today that Forrester Research Inc. has recognized it as a Leader in The Forrester Wave™: ECM Transactional Content Services, Q3 2015 report. OnBase by Hyland has been listed among Leaders who offer mature ECM suites through strength of architecture, scalability and experience in transactional use cases.

In the report, Forrester notes:

  • Hyland’s vertical strategy has helped add to its base of 13,400 lifetime customers, a majority of which are focused on ECM capabilities for transactional services.
  • OnBase remains a practical and cost-effective choice with industry experience, especially in education and healthcare.
  • Providers are accelerating their move to the cloud via substantial investments in data centers and cloud infrastructure, filling gaps in their mobile road maps and improving process support for embedding smart process applications.    
  • Providers are addressing emerging ECM opportunities that include stronger and more open APIs.

“OnBase is an information hub that connects and optimizes systems, processes and operational units across the enterprise,” stated Bill Priemer, president and CEO at Hyland, creator of OnBase. “We believe that being cited as a leader in Forrester’s ECM Wave further validates the advantages of managing content, processes and cases in a single, configurable platform.”

Hyland is recognized for its innovative development with both mobile and cloud offerings as well as its strong integrations with leading line-of-business products, including Epic Systems within the healthcare sector and Guidewire within the insurance market. Forrester also noted that OnBase strengthened its low-code platform, which allows for rapid solution deployment.
