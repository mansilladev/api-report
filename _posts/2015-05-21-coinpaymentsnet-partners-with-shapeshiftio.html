ShapeShift.io, an instant Bitcoin and altcoin exchange service, has partnered with CoinPayments.net, a cryptocurrency checkout system for merchants, through the integration of the ShapeShift API. The integration powers CoinPayment’s platform to allow merchants to receive various cryptocurrencies and automatically trade them for their digital asset of choice.

"We are excited to offer simple and frictionless conversions to our customers using the ShapeShift API,” said CoinPayments.net CEO Alex Alexandrov, “This partnership gets us one step closer to a system where customer can pay in any currency they choose and vendors settle in what they find most convenient for their business."

Developed to be primarily used as an API service, ShapeShift’s integration with CoinPayments.net is one of many partnerships the instant digital asset exchange is building. CoinPayments proved an exceptional example of how the Shapeshift API can be utilized efficiently.

"CoinPayments is the leading merchant processing software for altcoins and has built substantial customer loyalty into their platform,” said ShapeShift.io Founder and CEO Erik Voorhees, “With ShapeShift now providing seamless conversion into Bitcoin, CoinPayments can scale faster and will automatically support the growing pool of ShapeShift's digital coins."

Outside of their API offering, ShapeShift offers a intuitive, user-friendly website exchange for direct consumer usage, where they can trade Bitcoin for other leading cryptocurrencies. For business interested in learning more about ShapeShift API integration, please contact emily(at)shapeshift(dot)io.
