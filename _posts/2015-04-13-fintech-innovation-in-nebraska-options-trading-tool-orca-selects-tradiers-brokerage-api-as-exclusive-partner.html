Tradier, Inc., the leading cloud-based Financial Services Brokerage API, today announced that it has integrated with Orca’s options trading offering.

Orca is an options trading tool providing every trader with cogent strategy. The company’s mission is to make option trading quicker and simpler - creating volatility or other options strategies in seconds.

“Orca’s mission is to reinvent the options trading tool for traders and investors with higher expectations. By integrating Tradier’s brokerage API we’ve continued that aspiration of delivering an exceptional trader experience,” said Greg Hammond, Founder and CEO of Orca. “Our clients now have access to cutting-edge technology and brokerage services with competitive pricing through Tradier’s innovative brokerage API platform.”

“We believe in Orca’s commitment to simplifying option trading to capture opportunity and efficiency. Our vision is to enable firms like Orca with our brokerage-in-a-box solution so that they can focus on creating world-class front-end user experiences,” said Dan Raju, CEO and Co-Founder of Tradier, Inc. “Orca is creating that experience and we are thrilled to be their exclusive brokerage partner and that this combination will continue to democratize access to superior trading execution for investors.”

With the integration of Tradier onto the Orca platform, users have the opportunity to act on Orca’s strategic analysis and execute their options trades without leaving Orca’s platform. Orca will also benefit from Tradier’s Service Desk and their customers’ low commission and fees due to the efficiencies driven by the Tradier’s use of Cloud Technology.

“Orca’s adoption of our brokerage API shows how an application can now easily use the API to build and launch trading capability for its users. This was impossible to accomplish effortlessly before Tradier,” added Raju.
