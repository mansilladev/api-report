ZH Healthcare (ZHTM) based in Washington, D.C., the creators of BlueEHSTM , a Health IT as a ServiceTM (HITaaSTM) platform announced that the Rural Institute of Medical Science(RIMS), an accredited medical college, will implement ZH's HITaaS platform for their 300 bed hospital and nursing school.

RIMS – (Rural Institute of Medical Science) is a project of Specialty Hospitals Group Pvt. Ltd. The RIMS Hospital at Nedumangadu, in Kerala, India, is a 300 bed hospital which has played a pivotal role in elevating the standards of healthcare in the region where their impact has been remarkable. Incorporated in 2003, RIMS is the prime choice of patients due to its state-of-the-art facilities and their qualified physicians. The hospital prides itself on its: 24-hour emergency department, critical care department, medical and surgical facilities, and an exceptional OBGYN department.

John G Kuruvilla, Managing Director, Specialty Hospitals Group, Pvt. Ltd., stated “Healthcare in Kerala is the best in India and is on par with some of the developed countries in the world. However, healthcare technology has yet to catch up. A world class cutting edge solution like BlueEHS-HITaaS will not only help RIMS but also elevate the level of technology in the state, consequently helping both patients and doctors.“

BlueEHS-HITaaS (Health IT as a Service) is now being used by over 5,000 users primarily from outpatient facilities from 65 countries. The demand for BlueEHS is on the rise, monthly sign-ups from doctors and practices are 400 and growing. The BlueEHS-HITaaS platform is now adding the hospital management systems to its vast library of modules, which include:

Modules:

  • Admissions and Registration
  • Laboratories
  • Radiology and Pacs
  • Pharmacy
  • Ward and bed management
  • Telemedicine-Video Stations
  • Medical/health Records
  • Nursing station
  • Doctors desk
  • Critical care/Emergency Medicine
  • Operation theater mgmt.
  • Patient Portal
  • Online Scheduling
  • Payment platform
  • Finance Interface(through APIs)

Shameem C. Hameed, Chairman and CEO of ZH Healthcare, stated that “With the addition of the hospital modules, BlueEHS-HITaaS will become the go-to-solution for hospitals and clinics because it is robust and available at a fraction of the cost for comparable systems. BlueEHS-HITaaS makes Healthcare IT easy and affordable which in turn will bring high-end healthcare technology to people all over the globe.”

Healthcare is an area where technology can bring lifesaving impact and governments globally recognize the need to better utilize technology to improve their healthcare systems. At the same time, health information technology is highly specialized within expertise, and compliance and regulatory frameworks must be followed. Thus, health IT can be intricate, complex, and costly requiring significant time and resources to develop a solution that properly accounts for all constituents.

Health IT innovators can use BlueEHS-HITaaS to address these many challenges. They need not reinvent tools to get their project off the ground, instead they can reconfigure the modules from ZH’s library which continues to grow both from ZH and users. Also, ZH already has agreements with laboratories, electronic prescription networks, clearinghouses, health information exchanges, and other entities that form a health information network, developing this network from scratch could take years. Further, the solution is delivered on a cloud based regulatory compliant platform. Finally, having configured the solution a health IT innovator can white label and commercialize their solution.

ZH Healthcare Partners from across the world have leveraged the platform to develop various solutions, examples include: a fully Arabic and regionalized platform for the Middle East, an EHR exclusively for the sleep study market, a health IT solution for the long-term care industry, a chronic care management patient engagement tool, and a video consultation application. Key among these is an EHR for outpatient clinics that is highly customizable with configurable treatment plans and clinical processes.

About ZH HealthcareTM
ZH HealthcareTM is committed to making health information technology and electronic medical records affordable and accessible to everyone. ZH does that primarily through a Platform as a Service (PaaS) that delivers Health IT as a Service(HITaaS). BlueEHS: HITaaS is a Freemium SaaS platform that empowers healthcare service providers, whether doctors or organizations, to quickly build and deploy their Health IT solutions using a set of tools and modules available on the cloud with minimal time and cost.

BlueEHSTM is established on a secure and regulatory compliant, scalable cloud based architecture, and has the convenience of a pay-as-you-go model.

BlueEHSTM is used by over 5,000 users in 65+ countries, and is adopted by over 400 additional practices each month.
