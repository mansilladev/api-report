Tradier, Inc., the leading cloud-based Financial Services Brokerage API, today announced that Screener.co, developer of a global equity research platform has added Tradier’s brokerage API to its platform.

Screener.co offers fundamentals data and profiles for more than 30,000 securities, with the ability to create, screen against, and view custom composite metrics using 10 years and 10 interim periods of financial statement history. This integration adds a fundamentals-based style of analysis and trading to Tradier’s comprehensive and expanding roster of technical tools.

“We produce transparent tools that give users full access to run analyses on the underlying data and allow them to come to their own data-driven conclusions,” said Lenny Grover, Screener.co founder and CEO. “With our adoption of the Tradier API, we’ve delivered a powerful combination to investors – accessible and actionable market data with Tradier’s low cost trading execution.”

“Tradier is accelerating a new brokerage model – with the aim of disrupting the traditional disjointed trading experience,” said Dan Raju, CEO and Co-Founder of Tradier, Inc. “Without having to leave their preferred platform, Screener’s customers have complete customization and they can access equity and options trading with superior execution at low cost pricing – some of the lowest pricing in the industry.”

The Tradier platform will make Screener’s customizable views─including more than 1,000 data points per company─ available to professional users as well as individual investors, at accessible price points. The partnership connects Screener.co users to Tradier Brokerage’s real-time streaming stock data and includes the advantages of personal one-on-one trading software consultancy and technical support.
