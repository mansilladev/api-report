CM First Group USA announces a move of all Americas personnel to a new, larger workspace at 7000 N. Mopac Expressway, Austin, TX, in the Plaza 7000 Office Building.

“The move brings the executive, sales, support and development teams together,” said John Rhodes, CTO. “The new space offers more conference rooms and meeting areas.”

The N. Mopac address is only a five-minute drive from downtown Austin, making it easier for customers to meet with CM First. Austin is a vibrant IT community; innovation is part of the culture of Austin.

Christoph Heinrich said, “CM First belongs in Austin. No other city offered as much for the CM First U.S. offices. The new space will encourage and promote communication across all teams and all levels of management.”

As a leader in software modernization methodologies and solutions, CM First always ensures that every aspect of the work experience fosters creativity. The new office space is designed to meet that goal. Learn more about CM First modernization here: http://www.cmfirstgroup.com/initiatives/application-modernization/

Find all CM First offices here: http://www.cmfirstgroup.com/company/contact/

About CM First Group
CM First is a global software and services company established in 1999 with a focus on modernizing applications, managing technical debt and the agile development of enterprise apps. Delivery areas include; migrations of software code, databases and IBM hardware, custom app, architecture and API development, mobility solutions and knowledge transfer. CM First works with hundreds of IBM and CA Technologies business-critical systems worldwide, with numerous reference customers covering industries as diverse as logistics, travel, insurance, food processing and oil and gas construction.
