WSO2 and IntegrationWorks today announced a strategic partnership to deliver integration solutions to clients in New Zealand that enable companies to drive down cost, maximize revenue, and mitigate risk.

WSO2 delivers the industry’s most comprehensive open source platform for building, integrating, managing, securing and analyzing companies’ APIs, applications, and Web services—on-premises, in the cloud, on mobile devices, and across the Internet of Things. IntegrationWorks helps clients use and share their information more effectively, save time, reduce costs, and improve application security in their business integration efforts.

Under terms of the agreement, IntegrationWorks will use WSO2 open source middleware to provide customers with systems integration services based on service-oriented architecture (SOA) best practices. Additionally, WSO2 will provide the partner company with technical, sales and marketing support to promote adoption and help customers achieve the high availability, high performance, and flexibility they require for IT and business agility.

About IntegrationWorks
IntegrationWorks is New Zealand’s leading integration solution provider, having developed a set of proven methodologies to analyze, design, implement, and support a variety of integration platforms. The company builds reliable, efficient integration solutions using service-oriented architecture, and can help with everything from architecture planning to ongoing monitoring and management. Whether customers need to optimize their business processes, securely link two businesses, or build a business-to-business gateway, IntegrationWorks ensures that integration initiatives deliver maximum value.

About WSO2
WSO2 uniquely delivers on the promise of the connected business. It offers the only completely integrated enterprise platform that enables businesses to build, integrate, manage, secure and analyze their APIs, applications, and Web services—on-premises, in the cloud, on mobile devices, and across the Internet of Things. Leading enterprise customers worldwide rely on WSO2’s award-winning 100% open source platform and its robust performance and governance for their mission-critical applications. Today, these businesses represent nearly every sector: health, financial, retail, logistics, manufacturing, travel, technology, telecom and more.
