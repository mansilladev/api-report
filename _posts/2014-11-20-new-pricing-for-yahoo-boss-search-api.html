Dear valued Yahoo customer, 

As you may have noticed on our website, and per our email notice sent to you on November 5, we want to make sure you are aware of an important change to our pricing for the Yahoo BOSS Search API suite of products, effective tomorrow, November 20, 2014. 

The changes are as follows:

  • Full Web will cost $1.50 per thousand queries 
  • Limited Web will cost $1.20 per thousand queries 

These pricing changes will be reflected in the charges you incur for those services starting tomorrow, November 20 and will not apply to charges incurred prior to that date. You accept these changes by continuing to use the BOSS services after the effective date. 

We sincerely appreciate your support. We remain committed to providing developers with tools for building custom, web-scale search engines and look forward to continuing to innovate and evolve the Yahoo BOSS Search API. 

For additional support, please visit our support page. 

Regards,

The Yahoo BOSS Search API Team
