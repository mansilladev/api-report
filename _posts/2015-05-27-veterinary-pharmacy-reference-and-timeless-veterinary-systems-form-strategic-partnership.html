Veterinary Pharmacy Reference On-line (VPR 2.0), a joint venture between BMC Software Development and CaerVision, has formed a partnership with Timeless Veterinary Systems Inc., the creators of the Vet Drug Index™, in order to develop and release a new web-based Veterinary Pharmacy Reference software: VPR 2.0.

VPR 2.0 will feature expanded PIM integration and improved API, while also providing enhanced software functionality, real-time drug database updates and immediate access to the VPR drug resource guide. A new training platform will provide support in the form of webinars, print collateral and a live support call center.

“The development of a web-based VPR solution will allow veterinarians to instantly access relevant information to educate pet owners on the medical treatment being provided for their pet,” said John Rowe, president of Timeless Veterinary Systems.

“The intention when creating VPR was to provide an easy-to-access, up-to-date reference for fellow veterinarians to improve the safe use of medications in veterinary medicine. VPR 2.0 will increase the ability to assist veterinarians, thanks to real-time updates and a robust online platform.” Teresa Koogler, DVM and founder of VPR.

About BMC Software Development
BMC Software Associates, founded in 2002, provides and supports Veterinary Pharmacy Reference as an electronic, interactive veterinary pharmacology. BMCSD provides semi-annual drug database updates to VPR and complete support for the VPR product. The company is part of the Business Management Corporation in Frederick, Maryland.
