The integration lets SutiExpense pay employee out of pocket expenses through ACH using the SynapsePay API.

SutiSoft Inc., a trusted provider of easy to use business management solutions, announces the integration of SynapsePay, the payment software company, with SutiExpense, its online expense management software platform.

SynapsePay is a payment network that makes it easier, more secure and less costly for businesses to send and received payments between bank accounts.

For more details about the solution, please visit http://www.sutisoft.com/sutiexpense/ or call us at 1.650.969-7884 or e-mail media(at)sutisoft(dot)com.
