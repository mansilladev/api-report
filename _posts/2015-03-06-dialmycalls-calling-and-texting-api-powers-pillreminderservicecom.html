DialMyCalls is one of the leading providers of voice and text message broadcasting. Companies now have the ability to tap into the mass notification platform with its calling and texting API. Developers can utilize the completely revamped API to easily integrate its capabilities into an existing service or create a completely new service which is exactly what PillReminderService has done.

Medications are extremely important as many are a matter of life and death. It is vital that everyone take prescribed medications as directed by a medical professional but unfortunately millions of people simply forget. Pill Reminder Service has devised an easy-to-use solution – customers can sign up to receive automated reminders when it's time for certain medications.

With the help of DialMyCalls and the calling API that they offer, PillReminderService.com is able to offer customers the ability to schedule and receive up to four pill reminder phone calls every day throughout the entire month. The days of forgetting to take important medication at the proper time are over.

“We have spent a lot of time developing an extremely powerful API and we are thrilled to offer everyone the ability to use our mass notification system,” says CEO David Batchelor. “The possibilities are endless when using our calling and texting API and it's great to have PillReminderService on-board to help folks with their medication.”

DialMyCalls' calling and texting API is available for everyone to use. Simply sign up for an account and you will immediately have access to an industry-leading mass notification system. Utilize a developer and the supplied API documentation to add automated notifications to any existing or newly created service. Send out church reminders, medication reminders, staffing alerts, emergency notifications, school alerts, and much more – the possibilities are endless when utilizing DialMyCalls.

About DialMyCalls
DialMyCalls.com was founded in 2006 and has quickly become one of the top voice and SMS text message broadcasting services available. The company has released a powerful calling and texting API that will allow companies such as PillReminderService.com to come to fruition. Tapping into the powerful mass notification platform will help create services that will help people in various niches – automated medication reminders is just the tip of the iceberg.

About PillReminderService
Creating a specific routine and finding the right solution for you or your loved ones to take their medication at the appropriate times is worth your attention and should be addressed before it does any damage to their health. It is clear that medication noncompliance is a widespread problem that takes a toll on millions of families and even the economy. Pill Reminder Service looks to create an easy-to-use platform that will help solve the medication noncompliance epidemic that affects millions.
