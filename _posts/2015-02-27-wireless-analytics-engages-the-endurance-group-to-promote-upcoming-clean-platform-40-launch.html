Wireless Analytics, an award-winning provider of enterprise mobility management services, and one of the founders of the wireless expense management industry, announced they have engaged business development firm The Endurance Group to promote the upcoming release of CLEAN 4.0, Wireless Analytics’ latest iteration of the CLEAN Platform™ (Communications Lifecycle Expense Analytics) mobility management suite of services. The Endurance Group, based in Yarmouth, ME specializes in business development support for new technology initiatives

“We’ve put over a decade of work, in terms of understanding our customers’ needs, into CLEAN 4.0,” said Carl Jahn, CTO and founding partner of Wireless Analytics. “CLEAN 4.0’s web tools have been purpose-built from the ground up using the latest technology to enable enterprise organizations to easily gain actionable insights from their mobility data.”

The CLEAN Platform™ is Wireless Analytics’ core product line, comprised of a cloud-based subscription application with high-touch customer support empowering enterprises to maintain complete visibility into mobile spend and usage across all major carriers. The CLEAN Platform™ can accurately allocate every penny of the enterprise’s mobile invoices down to the user level, providing a level of mobility insight that is unmatched in the industry.

Features being finalized for the Clean 4.0 release include: 

  • An updated, tablet-friendly interface
  • Easy, flexible integration with corporate intranets, including single-sign-on and corporate identity customization
  • Enhanced visibility into corporate mobility data at multiple levels of the organization, including advanced drill-down capabilities
  • Localization support, including language support and currency normalization
  • Tighter integration with 3rd party systems through REST-based API

“Making the REST API available to our clients expands visibility into our single pane of glass,” said Alex Mordach, Director of Engineering for Wireless Analytics. “The API integration will unlock a lot of new reporting flexibility for our customers. CLEAN 4.0 also features a full UX redesign. This release is on the forefront of technology, from the hardware it’s built on, to the carefully designed user experience.” 
CLEAN 4.0 is scheduled for release later this year.
