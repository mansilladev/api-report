As StrongLoop’s only partner with presence in Mexico, iTexico will host the event, which will be facilitated by StrongLoop Node.jr professionals. Developers from all over the country will be attending the three-day course to become certified Node.js developers for the StrongLoop platform. Each participant will receive a voucher for the certification and a complimentary StrongLoop teacher.

iTexico is proud of its partner network and its contribution to the Mexican developers community. Through iTexico’s liaisons with partners like StrongLoop, the company wants to grow awareness of the different technologies and platform tendencies, which can help the development of bright business opportunities for the entire Mexican software development industry.

“We’re are very excited about our new partnership with StrongLoop. We think that Node.js has great potential but is still being ignored by many. That’s why we created this training program. Through our nearshore presence in Mexico, we want to use this opportunity to bring more awareness of Node.js to the Mexican software development industry,” says Anurag Kumar, CEO of iTexico.

About StrongLoop: StrongLoop is based in San Mateo, CA. Their mission is to deliver expertise and software solutions to help developers succeed with their Node projects. StrongLoop is the largest corporate contributor to the upcoming Node v0.12 release and the current v0.10 release. This deep expertise is leveraged to develop the open source, LoopBack API framework, the StrongOps DevOps and monitoring console plus technical support, consulting, training and certification.

About iTexico: iTexico is a software services company that offers convenient mobile and cloud solutions and extended teams. From their headquarters in Austin, TX, iTexico provides the highest level of technical expertise delivered collaboratively and affordably to their customers from the company’s Delivery Center in Guadalajara, Mexico. iTexico has a strong commitment to supporting the local high-tech community by offering trainings and certifications that help young developers learn new skills and stay up-to-date with the newest trends.
