At Mashape, we support thousands of APIs. Over the years, we have learned a lot about different authentications and the complex world of OAuth. Today, we are happy to officially release Guardian.js, the open-source gatekeeper that removes the OAuth dance with a single request.

In the past, the community has shown lots of love and support for our previous open-source projects: OAuthBible and Unirest.io.

As we continue to develop new API tools, we’ll continue to share our work with the developer community!
