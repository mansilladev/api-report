We are back for a third  WIPJam @ mHealth Summit: Mobile Development on Sunday, December 7th, the day before the start of the Summit.  If is your choice; you can register for just WIPJam, just mHealth or both.

The WIPJam @ mHealth Summit will help you get the most out of mobile! Whether you’re new to mobile development or an experienced old hand, you will take away knowledge from our sessions to enhance your mobile strategy and improve your mobile apps.  Join us to learn why past  attendees called the WIPJam "the best part of the mHealth conference,"and said "This should have been part of the mHealth conference for all attendees!"

Sessions/Topics include:

  • State of the mHealth Industry
  • mHealth App Publishing - What it takes to become Successful!
  • Getting Started: From the Mobile Ecosystem to the Development Lifecycle
  • “10 Ways to Fail” with App Development
  • Beacons for Fun and Profit
  • Development Story: Mobile Virtual Reality
  • UnPanel: APIs & Big Data
  • UnPanel: App Testing
  • UnPanel: Wearables/IoT
  • UnPanel: Social Media
  • Gamification Impact on Health
  • and more.

The 3 main benefits you will get from attending WIPJam are:

  1. Clarity on mobile development environments and support on build, buy or partner choices.
  2. Introduction to key partners, suppliers and customers.
  3. Techniques in mobile development to make a good app – great!
