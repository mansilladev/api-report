We've just introduced a family of Aggregate Functions the Orchestrate API. With aggregates, you can finally see the big picture! Build dashboard charts and graphs, faceted product exploration, or anything else that relies upon a birds eye view of your data.

Orchestrate Aggregate Functions include:
  • Statistical Aggregates: Provides a summary of the numeric values in a particular field.
  • Range Aggregates: Aggregate summary of numerical data in your collection. Configure a collection of numeric ranges to calculate the number of field values that fall within the designated ranges.
  • Distance Aggregates: Calculate how many geo-locations in your dataset fall within a set of distance-range buckets from a central location.
  • Time Series Aggregates: Calculate date-time values in your dataset within various chronological intervals.
