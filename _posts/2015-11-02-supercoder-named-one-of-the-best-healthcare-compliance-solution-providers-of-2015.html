HealthCare Tech Outlook magazine named SuperCoder, a division of the Coding Institute, one of the top ‘20 Healthcare Compliance Solution Providers of 2015.’ The company received honors from a distinguished panel of CEOs, CIOs, VCs, analysts, and the Healthcare Tech Outlook editorial board, which annually reviews the top healthcare compliance companies and selects those at the forefront of tackling industry challenges.

The panel selected SuperCoder because of its capability to deliver effective solutions for healthcare compliance problems, “SuperCoder has created products that fit in the cloud-based trend, which can easily provide information to practices’ management, billing and record systems,” says Jen Godreau, VP of Development and Operations at SuperCoder. SuperCoder’s online tools and expert advice have helped numerous healthcare organizations resolve many different types of compliance issues.

SuperCoder continuously enhances its products to meet clients’ ever-changing needs and comply with healthcare regulations. The company’s BoneCoder10 is a coding compliance software tool for clients to seamlessly transition to ICD-10 by helping physicians and coders assign the correct ICD-10-CM diagnosis codes, incorporating ICD-10 guidelines, code combinations, and documentation tips. BoneCoder10 also provides a semantic search functionality, increasing efficiency and revenue. BoneCoder10 easily integrates with an Electronic Health Record (EHR), offering complete interoperability. HIPAAInstitute.com’s Policies and Procedures — a web-based HIPAA compliance plan and implementation wizard from SuperCoder — saves time because it includes a comprehensive repository of data for clients to put compliance into practice, avoiding increasing financial penalties for noncompliance.

SuperCoder offers many other compliance solutions for medical coding and billing, which include ICD-10 eLearning and Batch Scrubber. ICD-10 e-learning courses help medical practices keep abreast of ICD-10 medical codes and guidelines with interactive online presentations, walk-through examples, and documentation tips. Batch Scrubber, an automated tool that checks providers’ insurance claims for accuracy before they are billed to insurance, offers clients dashboards of claims compliance risks and allows clients to adjust those claims, reducing denials and speeding cash flow,“Many insurers and EHRs are using the compliance solution which links code errors to regulations and analysis as their back end system using Application Program Interface (API),” says Jen Godreau.

SuperCoder’s compliance solutions also include more than 30 monthly coding and compliance newsletters, or Coding Alerts,which deal specifically with Medicare regulations and how they apply to specialties ranging from Anesthesia to Urology, “The specialty focus helps customers quickly understand how regulations impact their business,” Jen Godreau says.
