HappyDesk and DavinciMeetingRooms.com dually announce enterprise integration. Shared spaces everywhere now have the ability to offer streamlined service and space management with a single calendar as well as sync live inventory between both enterprise platforms.

“The integration between HappyDesk and DavinciMeetingRooms.com enhances shared space service and space management. Shared space providers never need to worry about double bookings. DavinciMeetingRooms.com’s bookings and requested amenities will now appear on HappyDesk’s Master Calendar feature,” says HappyDesk’s Director of Engineering, Jo Vasev.

"We are excited to announce the successful completion of the API integration process with HappyDesk," says Martin Senn, COO of Davinci Virtual. Senn continues, "We continue to expand on our position as the industry’s leading meeting space reservation channel by offering live inventory integrations with major scheduling applications such as HappyDesk”

Launched in early 2014, HappyDesk has quickly transformed the way shared space are sold, accessed and serviced. Its peer-to-peer platform creates new opportunities for individuals and companies to transact and collaborate. Shared spaces seek HappyDesk’s software to increase revenue and maximize operational efficiency.

About HappyDesk 
HappyDesk's Sharing-space-as-a-service (Saas) software platform was created with a singular purpose – to enhance the human connection between shared space providers and seekers. Space providers leverage HappyDesk’s white label e-commerce, CRM, billing, proposal, door access and marketplace to increase engagement. Providers enjoy satisfied clients, increased revenue and greater efficiencies. Seekers rely on HappyDesk because of the endless amount of shared space options they can search and reserve.

About Davinci Virtual Office Solutions 
Davinci offers virtual office locations and virtual office services worldwide. With professional business addresses in over 1,000 locations, mail forwarding, lobby and directory listings, access to over 4,000 conference rooms and day offices, catering, administrative services, virtual assistants, business support centers, resident agent services, license hanging and more, Davinci Virtual helps businesses reach maximum potential with minimum overhead.
