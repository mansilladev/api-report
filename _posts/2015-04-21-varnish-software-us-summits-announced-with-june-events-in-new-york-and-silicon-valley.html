Varnish Software, the company behind the widely trusted open source web accelerator Varnish Cache, today announced that it will bring its popular Varnish Summit Series 2015, themed “Scaling the Heights,” to the United States this June. Registration is now open for Varnish Summits in Silicon Valley (June 2, 2015 atStanford Faculty Club) and New York City (June 4, 2015 at NYSSA).

Registration for both U.S. summits is open at: http://info.varnish-software.com/varnish-summits-2015-registration

Varnish Summit Silicon Valley

  • When:  Tuesday, June 2, 2015, 9:00 am to 5:00 pm
  • Where:  Stanford Faculty Club, 439 Lagunita Dr, Stanford, CA 94305
  • Link:  You can register for the Varnish Summit in Silicon Valley here. 

Varnish Summit New York City

  • When:  Thursday, June 4, 2015, 9:00 am to 5:00 p.m.
  • Where: NYSSA (New York Society of Security Analysts), 1540 Broadway, 10th floor (entrance on 45th Street—Times Square)
  • Link:  You can register for the Varnish Summit in NYC here.

Varnish Software powers the most demanding web sites across all industry types—for businesses such as the New York Times, Nikon, Transport for London and Vimeo as well as more than 2.3 million websites worldwide. The Varnish Plus commercial suite offers products for scalability, customization, monitoring and expert support services. Additionally, the newly released Varnish API Engine is Varnish Software’s high performing API Management solution that can handle up to 20,000 API calls per second.

The Varnish Summits offer users and partners the opportunity to engage with members of the Varnish Software team, together with special guests, customer speakers and Varnish Cache users from across North America.

“The Varnish Summits give all of us in the Varnish community an opportunity to come together, for learning and sharing how to get the most out of advanced applications of Varnish,” said Per Buer, Founder & CTO, Varnish Software. “The Varnish Summits focus on what’s empowering today’s highest performance websites to reach new measures of scalability. At the Summits in the U.S. we’ll announce a brand new addition to Varnish Plus, Varnish Cache Plus SSL backend support. Having Varnish support SSL without having to add a third party dependency will simplify the life of Varnish Plus users everywhere. Furthermore, we’ll explore new functionality of Varnish Plus in addition to presenting how you can future-proof your API strategy using our new product, Varnish API Engine,” Buer said.

For more details or to register for the Varnish Summits, read and subscribe to our blog. All summit information will be posted there, including speakers and agenda updates for the event. Tickets are limited, so register now for your free pass. 

About Varnish Software
Varnish Software delivers the world's most widely used HTTP accelerator based on open source. Varnish Cache was originally developed in Norway and has since spread to 2.3 million sites worldwide. It is currently used by some of the world's most influential websites such as The BBC, The New York Times, Business Insider, Morningstar, Vimeo, The Canadian Broadcasting Corporation and many more. Varnish Software's business model is to provide support, services and enterprise-level products for Varnish Cache via their primary product Varnish Plus.

Varnish Software has offices in New York, Los Angeles, London, Paris, Stockholm and Oslo. For more information, please visit: http://www.varnish-software.com/
