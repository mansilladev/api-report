ProcessMaker, the company that simplifies the building and automation of workflows and forms-based business processes for web and mobile apps, today announced its partnership with Bitnami, the leading supplier of pre-configured application stacks and development environments for local and cloud deployments. With this partnership ProcessMaker joins 100+ popular server applications available in the Bitnami Library, enabling one-click deployment on Amazon Web Services and the Google Cloud Platform.

ProcessMaker is the first to enter the BPM category of applications available in the Bitnami Library. With the Bitnami ProcessMaker Enterprise Edition package, organizations can easily set up new development and production environments of ProcessMaker in a matter of minutes, not hours, and only pay for what they use. Customers obtain a completely configured instance of ProcessMaker up and running on the cloud in just a few clicks – no “cloud” knowledge required.

“We’re delighted to be working with Bitnami,” said Brian Reale, ProcessMaker CEO. “Building and deploying applications in the cloud is becoming the norm, and we want to be sure to arm ProcessMaker admins with the most effective, streamlined platform to do so. ProcessMaker has always been known for making workflow and business processes automation simple to build and run. Our partnership with Bitnami now makes deploying to the cloud just as simple and easy. Our solution works transparently for web and mobile, so now organizations can literally be up and running with a solution to build custom mobile apps in minutes as well.”

“Bitnami is committed to helping software providers embrace the movement to the cloud, and we are excited to be able to provide improved cloud capabilities for ProcessMaker,” said Erica Brescia, Bitnami COO. “The Bitnami Software Partner program enables software providers to deliver a great deployment experience to millions of people. The Bitnami cloud launchpad technology and our partnership with leading cloud providers will help ProcessMaker realize the benefits of the cloud faster and easier.”

This announcement follows the GA launch of ProcessMaker Enterprise Edition Version 3.0, the latest release of ProcessMaker which includes a BPMN 2.0 compliant designer, new drag-and-drop HTML5 Responsive Web Design form designer, a powerful new REST API, and apps for IOS and Android.

The Bitnami ProcessMaker Enterprise Edition package is available immediately from http://www.bitnami.com/stack/processmaker.

About ProcessMaker
ProcessMaker is a leading cloud Workflow and BPM software suite that makes it simple for companies to automate form-based approval processes that interconnect people and data from existing company systems. Processes can run on the web and via mobile apps from the same ProcessMaker server. ProcessMaker is headquartered in New York and has a partner network spread across 35 countries and on five continents. Thousands of companies rely on ProcessMaker to run their processes. ProcessMaker is available in 17 languages and has been downloaded over 750,000 times. For more information visit http://www.processmaker.com.
