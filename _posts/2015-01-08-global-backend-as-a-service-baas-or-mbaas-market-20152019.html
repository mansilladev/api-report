BaaS, also referred to as MBaaS, is a unique model that enables mobile and web application developers to store and link their applications to a backend cloud infrastructure. BaaS is different from cloud computing services such as SaaS, PaaS, and IaaS. It uses custom SDKs and APIs, and provides various value-added features such as user management, usage analysis, and push campaigning or notification. It also enables seamless integration with social media networks such as Facebook and Twitter. In addition, BaaS is superseding the on-premises MEAP category and it provides lot more turn-key functionality for a mobile strategy than traditional API management and PaaS options. TechNavio's analysts forecast the Global BaaS or MBaaS market to grow at a CAGR of 101.88 percent over the period 2014-2019.
