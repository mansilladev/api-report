Making care more convenient, particularly for those with chronic conditions, is one of the most effective ways to lower hospital utilization, including inpatient bed days, length of stay, admissions, re-admissions and emergency visits. For many patients, there is nothing more convenient than using a mobile device to access health information and services. Axway (Euronext: AXW.PA), a market leader in governing the flow of data, announced today it will exhibit and present at the Healthcare Information and Management Systems Society’s(HIMSS) Annual Conference & Exhibition. Axway experts will discuss how to integrate, automate and secure information exchanges throughout the healthcare ecosystem, including on wireless and mobile devices.

Axway API Management, part of the Axway 5 Suite, can assist in the creation of a mobile patient experience that will capture and keep patients engaged, while improving patient care and outcomes.

Smartphones and iPads have proliferated among healthcare providers, giving them mobility and access to information instantly. Mobile technologies are used to help patients understand and follow their regimens of care and to educate and provide a medium for continued patient engagement. Axway’s mHealth approach is geared towards improving the patient experience by equipping doctors with a 360 degree view of the patient’s history, resulting in improved patient care.

With a wealth of education, networking opportunities and exhibits, HIMSS prepares attendees to stay at the forefront of change, utilize innovative solutions and meet their top-of-mind healthcare challenges. The conference will take place at the Chicago McCormick Place, Chicago on April 12-16, 2015. During HIMSS, Axway healthcare IT experts will share insights from their experience working with healthcare providers, payers and health information exchange purveyors to manage challenges faced and the growing regulatory demands of the healthcare industry.

HIMSS15 Knowledge Center – North Building, Hall B, Booth 18, Wednesday April 15, 10:45am – 11:15am CT

To share further insights on the impact of mobile and wireless technologies on the healthcare industry, Shawn Seaton, a lead mobile consultant at a leading healthcare maintenance organization will speak alongside Atif Chaughtai, Director, Healthcare Solutions, Axway in the HIMSS Knowledge Center. During the session entitled “Will mHealth be a Cure for Healthcare?”, Seaton and Chaughtai will share how an API Management solution aids in mHealth.

Attendees will learn: 

  • The top 4 strategies for mHealth
  • Hands-on experience and deployment lessons from a large healthcare maintenance organization
  • Best practices in enabling interoperability between electronic health records such as Epic Systems and mobile devices to meet Meaningful Use II
