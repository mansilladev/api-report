App Maker Appy Pie launches Form Builder API to allow developers can now build custom intuitive in-app forms by Adding forms with text fields, checkboxes, photo upload and much more. When clients need to collect information, learn people’s preferences or gather feedback, there’s nothing better than a form. By one simple form, clients can open their business to constant feedback, while still maintaining an air of privacy and professionalism.

Form Builder is an innovative API for generating various kinds of forms. The back end of the Form Maker is very easy to use allowing developers to create forms by simply Dragging & dropping fields to the form, with no need of programming skills and Possibility of sending submitted form to various emails.

“We detest complexity. That's why we didn't put any in our app builder. Appy Pie App Maker's pride lies mainly in the fact that it is extremely easy to use which is why 95% of our customers have built their custom applications all by themselves. All credits to the intuitive drag and drop interface, not only in form creation, but in the complete app building process.” said Abhinav Girdhar, Founder Appy Pie.
