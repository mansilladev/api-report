Payscape, a leader in financial technology specializing in payment processing, opened its newest office on the second floor of an old brewery located at 4125 Lorain Ave. Cleveland, OH 44113. This location allows Payscape to expand their merchant services and better service clients in the Ohio area.

Located above the Platform Brew Co. and in the city of the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, Payscape will make its mark in the FinTech industry. The new office will be lead by District Sales Manager, Jerry Hammer.

“I am extremely excited to be a part of a company with an already tremendous national footprint.” said Hammer. “Payscape’s continual growth and entrepreneurial attitude are what provides them with the ability to obtain the greatest levels of success in this commoditized industry. Team members are constantly learning new technologies, solving new problems, partnering and integrating with new verticals, and, most of all, thinking differently.”

Hammer is a Cleveland native and graduated from Xavier University. Before joining Payscape five years ago, Hammer worked for the The Hammer Wine Company, a family company. There he oversaw reps calling on restaurants, hotels and country clubs. Now Hammer serves as the district manager, managing business development, strategic partnerships and hiring efforts within Cleveland. The office is currently looking for more talent with the launch of their new office. Interested parties can apply online at payscape.com/careers.

“We are excited to open our office in the sprawling city of Cleveland” says Jeremy Wing, President and Co-Founder, Payscape. “Our continued growth and partnerships is a testament to our dedication to hiring and retaining the FinTech industry’s most talented people and partners, and we are excited to provide career opportunities as part of our expansion into the Cleveland area.”

Payscape has recently received national recognition as the Electronic Transactions Association’s, 2015 “ISO of the Year”. In addition, the company’s growth has been recognized by many influential organizations across the nation such as PYMNTS.com and Finovate. They have also been selected consecutively as a Top Place to Work for four years and named one of Inc. 500’s Fastest Growing Private Companies in America for the last six years.

Founded in 2004, Payscape is a leading integrated financial technology provider dedicated to making it simple for business owners to collect money. They are a team of inspired entrepreneurs intent on disrupting the commoditized payment landscape with cutting-edge products and services, universal API integration, best-in-class customer service, strategic referral and technology partnerships and a lifelong devotion to helping small to mid-size business owners increase cash flow.
