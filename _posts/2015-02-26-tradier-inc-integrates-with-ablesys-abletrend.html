Tradier, Inc., the leading cloud-based Financial Services Brokerage API, has announced its integration with AbleSys’ AbleTrend, a long-established risk management and chart-based trading platform.

The integration connects AbleTrend users to Tradier Brokerage’s real-time streaming stock data and includes the advantages of personal one-on-one trading software consultancy and technical support.

“With Tradier now available to our userbase, we now have the ability to immediately offer secure trading and investing from our application - delivering cost benefits, and the ability to trade quickly through multiple platforms including web, mobile or desktop,” said Dr. John Wang, CEO, AbleSys. “Tradier truly enables our service expansion, they don’t offer a competing product and they’ve created a seamless trading experience for our customers.”

“We are thrilled that AbleSys has adopted our open brokerage API and by leveraging Tradier’s cloud technology, their clients can now access low cost trading directly from the AbleSys platform through Tradier Brokerage. They share our vision to transform the investor and trading experience through groundbreaking technology and service,” said Craig Russell, SVP Product, Chief Evangelist, Tradier, Inc.

The recipient since 1997 of Stocks & Commodities Reader’s Choice Awards for stocks, futures, and options trading systems, AbleTrend supports 16 live workstations, each with up to 16 charts, allowing users to stay abreast of positions at a glance and provides perspective using multiple time horizons.

About AbleSys 
AbleSys trading software offers traders specific buy, sell, and stop signals that can be used for day trading, swing trading, and position trading of all markets, such as stocks, futures, commodities, forex & bonds etc.

AbleSys Corporation (ASC) is a worldwide leader in universal financial trading software and web application. As the creator of the benchmark ASCTrend indicator, AbleSys has long been synonymous with cutting edge financial trading technology. AbleSys strives to develop innovative products and services that meet users' evolving need in the Internet age. Founded in 1994, AbleSys is a Commodity Trading Advisor (CTA) registered with the Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC), specializing in trading software and internet applications. Products include AbleTrend, ASCTrend, and WinTic.
