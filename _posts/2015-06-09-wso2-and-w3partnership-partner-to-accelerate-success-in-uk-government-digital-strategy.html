WSO2 and W3Partnership today announced a continuation of their strategic partnership to deliver services and solutions on the Government Digital Marketplace, also commonly known as G-Cloud.

The partnership was announced in conjunction with WSO2’s news at WSO2Con Europe 2015 today that the company has achieved a 155% growth in partner-driven revenue in Q1 2015 over Q1 2014. W3Partnership is a Silver Sponsor of WSO2Con Europe 2015.

Commenting on the announcement, Marcus Langford – Chief Operations Officer at W3Partnership said, “The strategic partnership has already delivered eight contracts as part of the UK Government drive to achieve 25% spend on IT services through small to medium size organizations via its digital strategy. W3Partnership and WSO2 have proven they are uniquely positioned to provide a comprehensive, integrated platform fit for the requirements of tomorrow’s digital government services.”

Enabling Digital Government Services
WSO2 provides the industry’s most comprehensive open source platform for building, integrating, managing, securing and analyzing companies’ APIs, applications, and Web services—on-premises, in the cloud, on mobile devices, and across the Internet of Things. W3Partnership delivers highly focused specialist middleware cloud services and solutions based on a patterns based approach that melds the requirements of the modern digital economy with existing legacy solutions.

Under terms of the agreement, W3Partnership will use WSO2’s open source middleware to provide UK central, local, executive agencies and non-departmental government bodies with platform as a service (PaaS) and specialist cloud services (SCS). Additionally, WSO2 will provide W3Partnership with technical, sales and marketing support to promote adoption and help customers achieve the high availability, high performance and flexibility they require for IT and business agility.

“Through our partnership, mutual customers gain the deep integration and cloud expertise of W3Partnership along with the flexibility and power of our comprehensive, cloud-enabled WSO2 platform,” said WSO2 Vice President of Channel Sales Lindsey Anderson. “We look forward to building on our success in working with W3Partnership to help governmental organizations of all sizes take advantage of the agility, collaboration and efficiencies of the cloud.”

About W3Partnership

We provide expert services and solutions associated with delivering integration projects specializing in assisting customers to build a secure, robust integration platform for their internal or digital requirements.
