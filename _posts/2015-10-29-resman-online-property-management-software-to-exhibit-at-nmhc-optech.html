ResMan, online provider of cloud-based software and services to the multifamily housing industry will exhibit at the National Multifamily Housing Council’s OPTECH Conference and Exhibition. NMHC OPTECH, the premier destination for “what’s next” in multifamily, will take place at the Hilton San Diego Bayfront from November 17 to November 19 in San Diego, California.

ResMan® is the fastest growing online property management software solution in the multifamily industry, providing easy-to-use automation tools that improve operational effectiveness and productivity. ResMan features an intuitive user interface and workflows designed by active property management professionals who understand the user’s pain points and address these problems in efficient and cost effective ways. The accounting and operations suite offered by ResMan enables management to focus on potential areas of concern before they become a negative cash flow reality. ResMan’s BoardRoom modules show real-time data for overall property performance including occupancy, financial health, leasing activity, renewal efforts, make-ready turns and work orders.

Conference goers are invited to visit with ResMan executives at Booth #423. “At ResMan we don’t have customers, we have business partners. We work together to ensure you have the right solutions to successfully manage your portfolio. With a Free and Open API, ResMan provides an environment that allows users the benefits of “best of breed” selection for their product while still providing service needs under one ResMan umbrella,” states Kathie Cook, Regional Vice President of Sales.

About ResMan
ResMan provides cloud-based property management tools that enhance productivity and profitability of multifamily housing operators and owners. The SaaS provider focuses on Service First in everything, with intuitive software and industry’s leading customer experience. ResMan offers solutions for conventional multifamily and student housing providers, as well as on open API actively promoting a best-of-breed solution to meet each customer’s unique needs.
