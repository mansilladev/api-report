SoundCloud and Twitter have partnered to deliver the ability for SoundCloud powered audio cards in Twitter user timelines to share music and audio directly from their iOS and Android devices.

Currently the feature is available to a small group of music & audio creators to begin with, including This American Life (@thisamerlife), NASA (@nasa) and The White House (@whitehouse), NPR (@NPR), and Portugal the Man(@portugaltheman).

In the coming weeks, the cards will be available to any SoundCloud user, turning each Twitter user’s timeline into a social radio channel, going beyond just images and video--allowing music, podcasts, and other audio to exist side by side with each 150 character Tweet.
