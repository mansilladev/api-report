Trumpia, the leading mobile messaging and cross-channel marketing automation software provider, today released 3 new updates.

The first update allows users to send appointment reminders through any business phone number as well as a short code. Users can now send appointment reminders through text, email, mobile app, and voice broadcasts, letting them cater to their customers’ channel preferences.

The second update is called Text-to-Speech. It enables users to type a message that gets converted into a synthesized voice broadcast by Trumpia’s software. This is a great option for users who don’t have the means, or simply don’t want to record their message.

Lastly, in efforts to strengthen their security, Trumpia introduced new requirements for account passwords. Moving forward, all passwords are required to have 1 uppercase and 1 lowercase letter, one number or special character, and cannot be a simple word from the dictionary. Also, accounts will only allow a certain number of login attempts with incorrect passwords.
