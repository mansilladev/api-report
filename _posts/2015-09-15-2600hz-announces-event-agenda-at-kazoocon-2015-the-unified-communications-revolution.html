2600Hz, the leading Unified Communications platform has announced the agenda for KazooCon 2015. The event will be hosted on October 5th – 6th at Broadway Studios in San Francisco, CA. Early-Bird tickets are available for purchase for a limited time. Click here.

Keynote speakers will include 2600Hz CEO Darren Schreiber, COO Patrick Sullivan, and CTO Karl Anderson. Other speaking topics from 2600Hz will include Tuning Kazoo for 10,000+ Handset Installations; Rating, Routing and Service Plans; Detecting and Managing VoIP Fraud; and Managing Billing and Invoicing Efficiently.

Guest keynote speakers will include Lon Baker, COO at VirtualPBX; Christos Kolias, OpenFlow/ SDN Technical Lead at Orange; and David Messenger, Founder and CEO of Mast Mobile. Other speakers will include Daniel Constantin Mierla, Co-Founder of Kamailio; Michael Jerris, Co-Founder of FreeSWITCH; Greg Hobson, Market Development at IBM Cloudant; Mikhail Rodionov, Founder and CEO of SIPLABS; and Vernon Keenan, Founder and CEO of Telnexus.

2600Hz will also include in-depth demo’s that include the Future and Power of Video, WebRTC and Mobile; Onboard Customers Automatically via Your Brand; Provisioning including Encryption, NAPTR, SRV Records, Failover and Security; WebHooks and WebSockets - Getting Real-Time Information to Create Call Recording and Screen Pop Integration Tools; and Debugging using Packet Captures, Wireshark and NAT.

Inspired by Kazoo’s open-source VoIP platform and hosted by 2600Hz, KazooCon brings together developers, managed service providers, carriers and telecom evangelists to create unified communications systems. Announcements will include 2600Hz’s new reseller platform, Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) and Cluster Manager.

To get an understanding of KazooCon, watch last year’s video presentations.

Kazoo Training
Following KazooCon, 2600Hz will host an official Kazoo training October 7-9. This three-day training will teach software engineers about Kazoo and the third-party components that power the platform. Attendees will deep dive into Kazoo APIs and learn how to set up a cluster, GUI, WhApps, FreeSWITCH, BigCouch and more. To register for the Kazoo training, click here.

Company Information
2600Hz modernizes the way businesses communicate with a Unified Communications VoIP platform to suit Small Businesses, Resellers and Enterprise clients. 2600Hz enables users to modernize their offerings, simplifying their day-to-day operations, and take control of their telecom solution. 2600Hz leverages a clustered architecture to build a carrier-grade telecommunications infrastructure, allowing businesses to increase profits and maximize value. Founded in 2010, 2600Hz is a privately owned company. For more information, please visit http://www.2600hz.com.
