Apigee® (NASDAQ: APIC), developer of an intelligent API platform for digital business, is elevating the company’s visibility in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) by participating in GITEX Technology Week in Dubai this week. GITEX Technology Week is billed as the third largest technology event on the planet, with over 130,000 technology professionals expected to attend.

International technology leaders coming to GITEX this year will be able to explore solutions to bring their businesses into the era of connectivity. Apigee will be demonstrating the Apigee Edge API management platform on the SAP Partner stand in Hall 6 and on the Kaar Technology stand in the Sheikh Rashid Hall throughout the event. Attendees can learn how to use APIs to securely share data and services across myriad devices and channels.

APIs are programming instructions that enable software applications to exchange data and “talk” to other applications; behind every mobile app, smart device and connected experience sits at least one API. The Apigee Edge API management platform enables organizations to securely deliver and manage APIs, with agility and at scale.

The GITEX event is taking place October 18-22; this year is specifically focused on the “internet future of everything.” The event combines conference sessions, networking and meetings for senior industry professionals, and it aims to give attendees access to new ideas and innovative solutions to define future digital strategies.

For more information on Apigee, visit Apigee’s website.

About Apigee
Apigee provides an intelligent API platform for digital business. Many of the world's largest organizations select Apigee to enable their digital business, including 23 of the Fortune 100, six of the top 10 Global 2000 retail companies, and five of the top 10 global telecommunications companies. Apigee customers include global enterprises such as Walgreens, Burberry, Morningstar, and First Data. Apigee is headquartered in San Jose, California and has over 400 employees worldwide.
