Plyfe, announced today the launch of its new free offering, which enables anyone to create and add interactive experiences to their blogs, social channels, email and web properties. Plyfe leverages popular mobile and web mechanics such as quizzes, polls and swipe features, enabling major corporations to create fun and powerful features for mobile, digital and social. Now these same tools are available to all via Plyfe’s no-cost platform.

Plyfe, which in the past has focused on catering to enterprise level companies, has expanded its pricing model and introduced a subscription-based model that can match any budget. The free version is now available allowing anyone to signup and create interactive cards without big budgets or coding experience. Plyfe’s growing interactive card catalogue includes; Personality Quiz, Swipe Left/Right (Tinder for anything), Trivia, Timed Trivia, Registration, Image Based Polling, Video Trivia, Video Polling, # Galleries, Sweepstakes, Leaderboards, and more. An SMB package is also available with additional features for $100 per month.

“With our new free platform anyone with a social or digital presence can create fun and interactive dialogues for digital audiences” said co-founder and CMO, Jeff Arbour. “We are now making it faster and more cost effective for anyone to create engaging experiences that result in unique 1st party audience data.”

How It Works: 

  • Choose size and interactive card from Plyfe catalogues 
  • Input text or upload images, videos and/or gifs 
  • Publish anywhere

Currently Plyfe has more than 25 clients, including IBM, USA Today, Aetna, Ogilvy, Sports Desk Media, Unilever, Toyota and Campbell's. According to recent case studies, Plyfe’s interactive cards have seen an average of 46 percent user engagement, a 71 percent completion rate and a 21 percent sharing rate.

"The Plyfe platform has helped our customers drive engagement and uncover valuable audience insights across mobile, email, web, and social channels” said Eric Fernandez founder and CEO of Sports Desk Media. “Plyfe’s new pricing approach gives any person or business the opportunity to create fun and effective interactive experiences for their owned and paid media. Clients with smaller teams and budgets now have access to ‘big budget’ tools which has leveled the playing field.”

About Plyfe
With the Plyfe Platform anyone can create interactive cards in minutes with no coding required. Plyfe cards are embedded into existing mobile and web experiences, distributed via any ad server and easily added to social channels and email. Completion of Plyfe cards create unique data sets and Plyfe’s data API can easily integrate with pre-existing systems (DMP, CRM, Email, etc)
