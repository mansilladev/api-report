AddShoppers, Inc., the fastest-growing onsite marketing platform, today announced the AddShoppers suite of applications is fully integrated into the Demandware Commerce Cloud, the industry-leading enterprise cloud commerce solution from Demandware, Inc. AddShoppers’ LINK cartridge allows Demandware merchants to launch the AddShoppers platform, including the Social Login, Smart Sharing Buttons, and Behavioral Targeting campaigns, through just a few snippets of code. AddShoppers’ platform has been proven at scale to boost conversion rates, capture more customer data, increase customer referrals, drive up average order value, and more.

The integration leverages Demandware’s open commerce APIs to expose commerce functionality at the services layer and extend it to the AddShoppers platform. It allows for onsite trending product walls, Refer-a-Friend, and social contests through a simple configuration and connection process. Because all Demandware clients operate on a single code base, the pre-built integration has proven compatibility with the Demandware Commerce Cloud.

“Both AddShoppers and Demandware are data-driven companies with a laser focus on client success. We’re excited to empower Demandware clients with new world-class onsite marketing functionality that enhances the customer experience across channels. This partnership further strengthens our commitment to bringing the best clients the best products made by the best people", said Jon West, Co-Founder and CEO of AddShoppers.

“The team at Demandware is happy to announce the certification of the AddShoppers integration as part of the Demandware LINK partner ecosystem” said Tom Griffin, SVP, corporate development, Demandware. “Our customers will now be able to take advantage of this integrated platform to drive new social engagement.”

AddShoppers has experienced rapid growth since its founding in 2011, with the suite of applications being constantly improved upon and enhanced for enterprise use. Other features include:

  • Applications work with any platform, with minimal copy and paste integration required.
  • Asynchronous JavaScript hosted on Akamai’s Global CDN for maximum stability
  • Robust REST API for custom data integration
  • Fully managed in-house
