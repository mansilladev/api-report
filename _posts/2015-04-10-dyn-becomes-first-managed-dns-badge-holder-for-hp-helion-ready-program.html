For enterprise businesses, website outages or slow website performance hurts where it counts -- revenue, reputation and customer retention. That’s why Dyn, the worldwide leader in Internet Performance, announced today that it is the first DNS (Domain Name System) solution on the HP Helion Ready Program, which helps companies build and manage hybrid cloud services at enterprise scale. The announcement gives Dyn Managed DNS the HP Helion Ready Program badge, certifying the cloud-based solution is a technology that can be confidently deployed by the program’s customers.

Enterprises rely on top Internet Performance in a 24/7 environment to connect with customers, prospects, members, employees, and more. Outages and slow performance impact an organization’s bottom line and reputation. Dyn Managed DNS uses a global Anycast network with 18 points of presence (POPs) including India, Brazil, Australia and Hong Kong to ensure that a site’s users are reliably and quickly connected to the resources they need, delivering a superior user experience. Dyn’s Managed DNS offering includes disaster preparedness, security, API integration, DNS reporting and customer service around the clock.

Built on the foundation of OpenStack technology, the HP Helion Ready Program looks to boost the productivity of enterprises as they move to any cloud environment, whether public, private or hybrid solution. Taking an open, agile and secure approach to this industry movement, the Helion Ready Program connects organizations to choose from a vast global ecosystem of hardware, software and services as they deploy and scale products across the world.

“Enterprises are turning to OpenStack technology and hybrid IT delivery to scale their businesses, and with the HP Helion Ready Program, HP is making a significant contribution to this movement,” said Kyle York, CMO, Dyn. “As the first DNS solution to earn a badge on the HP Helion Ready Program, we are proud to help enterprises optimize their Internet Performance by ensuring the best possible user experience and therefore a stellar brand reputation by using Dyn Managed DNS.”

For more information on Dyn Managed DNS and the HP Helion Ready Program, please visit: http://dyn.com/hp-helion/
