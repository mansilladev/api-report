We keep adding new APIs to MeaningCloud to help you extract meaning from unstructured content in various business scenarios.

Corporate Reputation analysis is one of them. Customers wish to understand the public's perceptions around certain dimensions of their companies: financial situation, quality of products, social responsibility... And they need to extend the analysis to the huge volume of spontaneous opinions that users express in social media.

MeaningCloud's new Corporate Reputation API enables to factor any text taken from social networks, forums, blogs, news, etc. into the analysis with a high level of granularity and precision, in real time. Read the documentation here.

In addition, we used the opportunity of this MeaningCloud update to migrate two APIs from our previous product, Textalytics:

  • Lemmatization, PoS and Parsing. Performs linguistic analysis in several languages, determining the lemma and the morphosyntactic analysis of each word (Part of Speech tagging), and the syntactic analysis of sentences based on constituents. Read more..
  • Text Proofreading. Automatically detects spelling, grammar, and style mistakes in several languages. Read more.


Those of you who are still using the APIs of our former service Textalytics now have more reasons to definitely move to MeaningCloud. The migration is immediate following these simple instructions.
