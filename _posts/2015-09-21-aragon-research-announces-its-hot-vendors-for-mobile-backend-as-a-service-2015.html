Aragon Research, a technology focused research and advisory firm committed to providing thought leading strategic research and trusted advisory services, published its Hot Vendors for Mobile Backend as Service, 2015 today. The report highlights three MBaaS vendors.

Jim Lundy, CEO and Founder of Aragon Research, commented, “Mobile apps are changing the way we conduct business, but the secret sauce to having a great mobile app is having a Mobile Backend Service. MBaaS Providers are helping to power next-generation mobile apps that leverage both legacy and cloud services.”

The 2015 Hot Vendors for Mobile Backend as a Service are helping enterprises leverage investments in legacy apps and other services, such as the Internet of Things. MBaaS platforms allow developers to easily link via SDKs and APIs to a cloud-based backend for mobile and web apps.

Though the Hot Vendor Report provides extensive analysis and insight, it is not intended to be a complete list of vendors in the MBaaS market. Rather, it highlights vendors with interesting, cutting-edge products, services or technologies. For the full analysis of the three Hot Vendors, read the full report: https://aragonresearch.com/hot-vendors-in-mobile-backend-as-a-service-2015/.
