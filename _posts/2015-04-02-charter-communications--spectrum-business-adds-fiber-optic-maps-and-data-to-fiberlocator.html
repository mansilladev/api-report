FiberLocator, a proprietary fiber infrastructure and colocation data source from NEF, announced the addition of new data from Spectrum Business. A division of Charter Communications, one of the largest cable, Internet, and voice providers in the United States, Spectrum's nationwide fiber footprint and current lit building maps are now easily searchable through FiberLocator's subscription service. The fiber optic maps and lit building information quickly and easily aligns businesses with the connectivity they need, all through a streamlined and well-managed interface.

"Spectrum Business Partner Program prides itself in making it easy for agents to sell our products and services and then process the orders quickly and efficiently," said David Neely, Spectrum Business Director of Channel Sales and National Accounts. "With our new business relationship with NEF, this will further assist our channel sales agents in targeting opportunities on and near our rich fiber network."

Steve McCarthy, COO of FiberLocator's parent company NEF, added, "We're pleased to be getting more and more cable data in FiberLocator. Spectrum Business partners will benefit from the increased visibility to many new potential clients in need of their services, and we recognize that cable providers are playing a bigger role in providing business services. We believe it to be a win-win solution for both Spectrum Business and FiberLocator."

The data in FiberLocator is continually updated, with new information published each week. This robust knowledge base spans the entireUnited States and serves as a vital tool for commercial developers, corporations, and other technology-focused organizations that rely on network connectivity to drive business.

FiberLocator is currently accepting new fiber optic maps, lit buildings, and data centers for inclusion in its database at no charge to carriers. To learn more about adding carrier network maps and data centers to the database service, or to explore the FiberLocator API, visit FiberLocator.com or call (877) 239-3043.

About FiberLocator 
FiberLocator is a unique telecommunications data source mapping hundreds of thousands of "lit" buildings, data centers, and street-level facilities based carrier networks in the United States. The tool is available in a variety of formats, including an easy-to-use online application, application programming interface (API), and a customized platform-as-a-service solution. FiberLocator was created by the fiber and colocation experts at NEF, Inc., one of the nation's top telecommunications and data center consulting and brokerage firms. FiberLocator continues to grow as one of the most trusted central repositories of telecom infrastructure data, maps and intelligence. For more information, visit FiberLocator.com.

About Spectrum Business 
Spectrum Business, a division of Charter Communications, Inc., provides business organizations with scalable, tailored, and cost-effective broadband communications solutions, including business-to-business Internet access, data networking, business telephone, video and music entertainment services and wireless backhaul. Catering to the unique broadband needs of business customers, Spectrum Business offers competitively priced bundled products over its state-of-the-art, fiber-based network, helping businesses in a variety of industries maximize efficiency while continuing to grow. Spectrum Business is Metro Ethernet Forum Certified. More information about Spectrum Business can be found at business.spectrum.com.
