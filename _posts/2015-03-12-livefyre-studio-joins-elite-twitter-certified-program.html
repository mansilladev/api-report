Livefyre, the leading real-time content management and engagement platform, today announced that Livefyre Studio has been accepted into the exclusive Twitter Certified Program. The program recognizes outstanding services that help businesses utilize Twitter’s platform at its maximum potential.

Livefyre Studio, which the company launched in November 2014, is the only content management platform built exclusively for user generated content that lets marketers collect, manage, design and deliver custom real-time experiences without having to write a single line of code. As a Twitter Certified Product, Livefyre Studio now has full access to Twitter’s API’s, ensuring that Livefyre customers can easily search through every piece of Twitter data about a specific brand, topic of event and display only the best user generated content.

The Twitter Certified Program helps organizations discover the best products that will make them more successful on Twitter. Products invited into the program first endure a rigorous selection process, in which their functionality, technology, financials and customer satisfaction are extensively vetted. Only the leading products are selected, and as members of the Twitter Certified Program, they’re considered strategic partners to Twitter.

“Twitter is the number one social content curation source requested by our customers and a core part of the Livefyre experience,” said Jordan Kretchmer, founder and CEO of Livefyre. “We’re thrilled to add Studio to our list of Twitter Certified Products, as it signifies a continuation of our strong and long-standing partnership with Twitter.”

Livefyre Studio is Livefyre’s second product to be accepted into the Twitter Certified Program. Storify by Livefyre joined the program in May 2014.

About Livefyre 
Livefyre helps companies engage consumers through a combination of real-time content, conversation and social curation. The new Livefyre Studio puts billions of pieces of content at a marketer’s fingertips and lets them integrate that content into their digital marketing assets, websites, and mobile apps to increase traffic, user engagement and revenue. Livefyre is powering real-time content marketing experiences for over 1,000 leading brands including AOL, Bravo, CBS, Conde Nast, Cox Media Group, Dow Jones/WSJ, FOX Sports, Mashable, NASCAR, Showtime, Sony PlayStation, Sports Illustrated, Unilever and Universal Music Group. Livefyre acquired social storytelling platform Storify in September 2013 and social application provider Realtidbits in November 2013.

Founded in 2009 with offices in San Francisco, New York, London and Sydney, Livefyre was named one of the best places to work in the Bay Area by the San Francisco Business Times two years in a row and 2013 Corporate IT Software Company of the year by the World Technology Network.
