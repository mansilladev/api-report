RentPaidOnline is excited to announce it has completed its integration to MRI Software’s new API, increasing functionality and ease-of use for their shared clients. “This integration greatly simplifies the transfer of critical, time-sensitive data between RentPaidOnline and MRI Software’s customers,” said Steven Foster, EVP, General Manager of RentPaidOnline.

RentPaidOnline is one of the first to complete this level of integration with MRI Software and incorporates its comprehensive rent payment options, which in addition to eCheck and Credit Card payments, includes RentByCash® PayByText®, and AutoPay.”

Brett Moss, MRI’s Manger, Partner Connect said “MRI has been working closely with our partner, RentPaidOnline, to test an API integration between our solutions. MRI is excited to announce that RentPaidOnline has completed testing of that integration, and is in the process of deploying that integration with some of our mutual clients!"

About RentPaidOnline
RentPaidOnline's technology includes a real time web-based and mobile platform for electronic checks, credit cards, and cash payments for the property management industry. RentPaidOnline® is part of EFX Financial Services, a payments processor with almost ten years of payments experience, serving a wide range of industries with Rent Payment, ACH, credit card, payroll and ATM processing solutions. EFX Financial Services corporate office is located in Clearwater, FL. EFX Financial Services also has additional offices in Saint Augustine, FL., Atlanta, GA., Dallas, TX.,Portland, OR. and Philadelphia, PA.
