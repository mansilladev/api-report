Location, Inc. reported today that Cap Index’s lawsuit against Location, Inc. - a rival crime risk data provider - has been dismissed with prejudice in the federal district court of the Eastern District of Pennsylvania. The suit was brought by Cap Index against Location, Inc. in July of 2013 (Civil Action No. 13-cv-04225-CDJ).

“The suit has been completely dismissed. The terms of the settlement are confidential, but we felt strong about our legal position from the beginning and are very satisfied with the outcome,” said Andrew Schiller, CEO of Worcester, Mass-based Location, Inc. “We are glad that this suit is behind us so that we can continue competing in the marketplace, and offering businesses a choice in products."

Location, Inc.’s crime risk product is SecurityGauge®, which provides instant, objective crime risk reports for any address in the U.S., Puerto Rico, and the U.S. Virgin Islands.

Customers of SecurityGauge include national retailers, banks, property/casualty insurers, security providers, and other industries. SecurityGauge is available in PDF reports by address, via API, and is also available for large batch processing.

About Location, Inc.
Location, Inc. is a leading builder and source of location-based data and tools for risk analysis for corporate users nationwide. Many of the company’s technologies are patented, patent-pending, or proprietary, and have been refined over a decade of research and validation.
