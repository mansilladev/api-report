Chicago­based DragonSpears, a spin-off of Adage Technologies which has been providing web, mobile and e-commerce technologies for fifteen years, announces the opening of business for the company nationwide. Just when many large and middle market organizations are starting to hit the wall in terms of being able to keep up with the various data sources and demands on their workflows, DragonSpears is opening its doors to breathe new life and efficiency into the web and mobile application development space.

“Slaying and taming these beasts takes a focused mindset and a specific set of weapons,” explains new DragonSpears President Patrick Emmons. “We believe the true corporate heroes are the process designers, but they are being drastically underserved by traditional web and app programmers who do not truly focus on the workflow's success factors and efficiencies. DragonSpears was launched to help these professionals turn their workflows into game-changing web and mobile applications, providing a competitive advantage and unleash untold productivity.”

The new firm partners with engineers, process improvement managers and IT directors to offer workflow configuration and process consulting, web-based and mobile application development, and “Data Island” and API integration into existing applications and reporting solutions.

At DragonSpears, the team uses its proven and unique “APP-DS” development process to first isolate the success factors and then develop and deploy an application that focuses on the business objectives, both during initial launch as well as for subsequent updates and releases. The process includes a “Prioritize” phase, where the DragonSpears’ team collaborates with the client organization’s team to create a leaner process by focusing on
the essentials needed to reach the ROI factors; that is prioritizing the development to deliver the ‘first step’ application, proof points, and phased builds.

For the workflow managers and directors, there can be incredible pressure to perform large tasks within small budgets and protracted development cycles. DragonSpears steps in and provides improved speed, compliance and scalability of both internal and customer facing workflow processes. Stakeholders work with DragonSpears to gather, customize and create a platform that efficiently and cost-effectively moves business forward.

Patrick Emmons, founder and President of DragonSpears, has built a company that leverages mobility and automation for the best end result.

Mr. Emmons, co­founder of Adage Technologies (2001­2015), is launching DragonSpears as a spin­off dedicated to developing custom applications. He and his team are passionate about clearly and simply communicating the unique abilities of web and mobile portals to this sphere of fast-growing businesses.

“This fills an important gap in the market right now. Adage Technologies is proud to have been the crucible for their focused approach to custom development for business workflows,” says Roy Chomko, President of Adage Technologies.

Businesses who are looking to improve their efficiencies, communications and insights but have been marred by past development partnerships, today is your day. Enter DragonSpears.

DragonSpears exists to rescue and resuscitate the internal and customer focused workflow of growing businesses through web and application development. DragonSpears was founded in 2015 by Patrick Emmons, formerly of Adage Technologies.
