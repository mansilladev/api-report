Brokerage API company Tradier Brokerage, Inc. today announced that mobile trading technology pioneer Trading Ticket has integrated the Tradier API into its TradeIt tool to enable users browsing content on financial media and mobile apps to place orders directly from a page or app.

“Tradier’s ability to offer seamless access to brokerage services is perfectly in line with our mission to offer flexibility to investors in today’s fast moving stock market,” said Nathan Richardson, Cofounder of Trading Ticket. “We are pleased to empower investors hungry for information with broker connectivity from any Web or mobile device.”

“TradeIt users are in the market for in-depth financial information and quick access to act on that information. We are pleased to be working with Trading Ticket to enable the seamless transition from idea to order,” said Craig Russell, Chief Evangelist and SVP of Product at Tradier, Inc. “This latest integration continues our mission of empowering fintech companies who are offering the power of choice to the modern mobile investor.”
