Symmetry Software, specialists in payroll withholding tax solutions for large employers and service providers, is pleased to announce the addition of Jason Mackin to the team. Jason will primarily focus on Symmetry Payroll Forms, specifically the hosted version of the product, and an API for the product.

Jason says, “I am excited to be working with such talented and motivated people in a great work environment!” Jason comes to Symmetry from Manpower, where he worked on Java Application development with various frameworks and web technologies.

A graduate of Arizona State University’s Ira A. Fulton School of Engineering, Jason obtained a degree in Computer Science. In his free time, Jason enjoys golf, tinkering with computers and other gadgets, and spending time with his family.

About Symmetry Software

Symmetry Software simplifies the payroll process with accurate and dependable payroll withholding tax solutions. From Payroll Point®, the local tax identifier, and Symmetry Payroll Forms™, electronic withholding forms, to the Symmetry Tax Engine® and PaycheckCity® Calculators, Symmetry Software’s products give you the right taxes, the first time®.
