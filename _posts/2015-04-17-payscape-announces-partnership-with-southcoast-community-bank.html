Financial technology provider, Payscape, has teamed up with “Silicon Harbour’s” Southcoast Community Bank, to provide local businesses with the latest financial products and services that will allow them to process payments, streamline their business operations through innovative software solutions and increase cash flow.

“We are ecstatic about the partnership with Southcoast Bank. The way they treat customers aligns perfectly with Payscape’s core values. Our financial technology product suite will help grow both of our businesses at a rapid pace and keep our new and current client happy!” - James Hynes, District Sales Manager at Payscape.

Both Payscape and Southcoast are long-time supporters of growing local business within the “Silicon Harbour” community. With this new partnership, they can impact the local community by providing new and established businesses, with the products needed to run a successful business. Whether they need to accept credit cards at their retail storefront, process payments on a mobile phone, operate an online shopping cart or electronically invoice clients, Payscape has solutions that will satisfy all of those cash flow needs.

“Like Southcoast Bank, Payscape has a passionate team devoted to helping businesses grow and prosper. We are excited about offering Payscape’s cutting-edge products and services and best-in-class customer service to our customers.” - Ben Seabrook, Executive Vice President and Chief Operating Officer at Southcoast Bank.

To learn more about payment solutions, visit payscape.com.

About Southcoast Community Bank
Southcoast Community Bank is a locally owned and operated business that has been dedicated to serving the financial needs of neighboring communities since 1998. Located in the vibrant Charleston, South Carolina, metropolitan area, Southcoast Bank is headquartered in Mt. Pleasant. Southcoast has ten locations in the Greater Charleston market, including offices in Charleston, North Charleston, Mt. Pleasant, Johns Island, Moncks Corner, Summerville, and Goose Creek. Southcoast Financial Corporation's common stock is traded on the NASDAQ Global Market under the symbol SOCB. For more information about Southcoast Community Bank, please visithttp://www.southcoastbank.com.
