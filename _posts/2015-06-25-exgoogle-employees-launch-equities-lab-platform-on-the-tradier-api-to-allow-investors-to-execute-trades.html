Tradier Brokerage, Inc., the cloud-based financial services brokerage firm, today announced that Equities Lab has integrated with the Tradier API to empower investors to seamlessly execute trades and combine additional portfolio monitoring capabilities with the strategic insight created at the platform. Equities Lab provides comprehensive tools for complex investors to take the “guess work” out of the stock market with deep fundamental and technical indicators. The Tradier integration empowers investors at Equities Lab to now immediately execute a trade through Tradier Brokerage.

Built from the ground-up by ex-Google Engineers, Equities Lab uses a next generation user interface (UI) to help investors with stock screening, back-testing, charting, and managing their trading rules. The integration with the Tradier Brokerage API will enable traders at Equities Lab to use market insights derived from the platform to seamlessly execute trades. Key features provided by Equities Lab includes:

  • Fundamental and Technical Analysis – Equities Lab provides a powerful hybrid approach to help investors really understand the market.
  • Powerful Equihack System – Equities Lab provides investors with the ability to analyze not only any ratio they want, but its change over time, its rank vs. a peer group the investor defines and the change of its rank vs. a peer group over time.
  • Analytics – The Equities Lab heat map and other analytics allows investors to sort and categorize by any variable of their choice, color by another variable, and size by a third variable.
  • Backtest – Equities Lab allows investors to back test anything, then really dig into why it works, or why it doesn’t.
  • Alerts and Watch lists – Equities Lab provides daily alerts on trades and alerts investors on any new stocks added or if current holding should be sold

“We continue to be humbled by the caliber of FinTech innovators that have joined the Tradier network,” said Craig Russell, Chief Evangelist and SVP of Product at Tradier, Inc. “Working with a forward-thinking company like Equities Lab is at the core of our digital empowerment and innovation mission. We are pleased to be providing our open and secure brokerage API to the community at Equities Lab. The integration not only empowers their investors, but enables Equities Lab to focus on their unique investor experience.”
